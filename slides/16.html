Message Chains v. Happened-Before
Different structures, but if you squint:
  • Message chains are a subset of happened-before
  • Happened-before is not a subset of message chains
Consider the two execution on the right:
  • The order relation induced by the message chains is included in the happened-before relation.
  • But there is no message chain relation between b1 and b6, even though b1 happened-before b6
Lamport (1978)
